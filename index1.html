Hand-woven jewelry, accessories, and home decor

Who We Are

Two Daughters Design creates jewelry, accessories and home decor in the "Nantucket" weaving style.

What we do

We create jewelry, accessories, and home decor as well as offer classes and workshops to teach others how to weave. We hand weave, carve and assemble every element of each piece; whether it be a basket, bracelet, or bassinet. Every piece is made in New England with natural materials.
